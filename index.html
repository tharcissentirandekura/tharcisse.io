Introduction In world history and evolution, every century leaves behind some valuable stories. The 21st century highlights the growing connectivity of devices and a shift to using machines in daily activities(Evans & Annunziata, 2012). In this increasingly interconnected digital landscape of the 21st century, the rapid advancement of technology has brought more opportunities and challenges to human beings regarding data security(Peters, 2019). This rapid evolution of technology has influenced the growth and accessibility of Artificial Intelligence. Nowadays, Almost every area is affected by the use of Artificial Intelligence (AI), which affects how people in these affected areas do their activities. However, the area that got and still needs much significant attention in this era is cybersecurity and encryption in general. As technology evolves, AI is developing quickly, giving companies hope for its cybersecurity application by protecting computer networks, database networks, and system networks from cyberattacks and data breaches.
